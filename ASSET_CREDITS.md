# Asset Credits

The game's visual and audio direction is user-created and directed by Kiril Bekulov.

All remaining runtime assets in `public/assets/` are user-created or user-directed using tools such as ChatGPT, Gemini, Photoshop, and Suno. This includes the Gabi character sprites, lantern Gabi sprite, old-lady NPC sprite, lantern pickup, robot sprites, city and tunnel parallax backgrounds, platforms, fences, house, water, items, hazards, exit door, and background music.

The game's art direction, character design, environment style, item language, and music choices are dictated by Kiril Bekulov's ideas.
